Privacy Policy – Velora

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Velora.

Velora is an AI-powered interactive storytelling platform designed to provide immersive and personalized story experiences. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Velora mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Authentication provider information (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)
  • User ID and account preferences

User Content

  • Story choices
  • Chat messages and prompts
  • Story sessions and saved progress
  • Character preferences

Usage Information

  • Device information
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Analytics data
  • Subscription and purchase status

Purchase Information

Payments and subscriptions are securely processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. Velora does not directly store your payment card information.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide AI-powered story experiences
  • Save and synchronize story progress
  • Improve app performance and personalization
  • Manage subscriptions and coin systems
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
  • Analyze usage and improve features

3. AI-Generated Content

Velora may use third-party AI services to generate interactive stories and character responses. User prompts and story interactions may be securely processed through AI providers in order to generate responses.

We do not sell your personal conversations or private story data to advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Data may be securely stored on cloud infrastructure and protected using encryption and access control methods.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In
  • Firebase Analytics
  • RevenueCat
  • AI service providers
  • Cloud storage providers

6. Subscriptions & Purchases

Velora may offer premium subscriptions, virtual coins, and in-app purchases. Purchases are managed through platform providers such as Apple App Store and Google Play.

7. Children’s Privacy

Velora is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Retention

We may retain account and story data for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Users may request account deletion at any time.

9. Your Rights

  • Correct your data
  • Delete your account
  • Request data export
  • Withdraw consent

10. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the app settings or by contacting the support team.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Velora after changes means you accept the updated policy.
